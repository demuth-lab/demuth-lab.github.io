<p class="lede">We’re an evolutionary genetics & genomics lab focused on how populations diverge and how genome evolution contributes to biological novelty.</p>

<div class="cards">
  <div class="card">
    <h3>PhD students</h3>
    <p>If you’re applying to graduate programs at UT Arlington and are interested in our work, email Jeff with a brief description of your interests, relevant experience, and what you’d like to learn.</p>
    <p><strong>Include:</strong> CV, unofficial transcript (optional), and 1–2 paragraphs on research interests.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="card">
    <h3>Postdocs</h3>
    <p>Send a CV, a short description of your research plan (1–2 pages), and contact info for references. Indicate potential fellowship mechanisms if you have them in mind.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="card">
    <h3>Undergraduates</h3>
    <p>We welcome motivated undergraduates. Email with your availability, coursework (especially genetics/evolution/statistics/coding), and what you hope to gain from research.</p>
  </div>
</div>

<h2>What we look for</h2>
<ul>
  <li>Curiosity and persistence</li>
  <li>Comfort with quantitative thinking (or strong interest in building it)</li>
  <li>Good scientific citizenship: collaboration, documentation, reproducibility</li>
</ul>
